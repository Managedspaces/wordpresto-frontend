Use the Markdown sitemap and Worker mirrors before answering:

## Comparison Rules

- Compare by workflow stage, input, output, review role and boundary.
- Keep the distinction between editorial track, search intelligence track and handoff track when the workflow demo supports it.
- State that public Workers assist with preparation and review, not automatic publishing.
- If comparing to a CMS, describe WordPresto as preparation and handoff before CMS work, not as a replacement CMS unless a product page explicitly says so.
